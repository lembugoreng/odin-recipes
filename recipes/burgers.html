Burgers

Description

A burger is a hearty dish that contains buns, lettuce, tomato, onions and a burger patty.

Ingredients

  • Toasted Buns/li>
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onions
  • Burger Patty of your choice

Steps

  1. Prepare all the Ingredients.
  2. Assemble the burger starting with the bun from the bottom./li>
  3. Then gently place lettuce, tomato and onions in that particular order on top of the bun.
  4. Grill your patty until the meat is cooked and top the current burger with it.
  5. Finally, place your other bun ontop of your creation.
