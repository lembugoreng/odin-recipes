Nasi Lemak

Description

Nasi lemak is a flavorful dish that contains coconut rice served on banana leaf and having multiple sides which are kacang goreng,ikan bilis,sambal and ayam goreng.

Ingredients

  • Coconut Rice
  • Sambal
  • Kacang Goreng
  • Ayam Goreng

Steps

  1. Prepare all the Ingredients.
  2. Plate the nasi lemak
